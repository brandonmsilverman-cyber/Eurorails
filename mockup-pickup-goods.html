Design Changes Summary
1. Selected demand highlight: Gold left-border + amber tint + "SELECTED" badge on goods matching a selected demand row
2. Unselected demand hint: Subtle green tint + clipboard icon on goods matching any demand card row
3. Larger buttons: 12px bold green buttons (was 10px plain), gold for selected-demand matches, dimmed when disabled
4. Demand card badge: Green "Available here" badge on demand card rows when at a city producing that good
